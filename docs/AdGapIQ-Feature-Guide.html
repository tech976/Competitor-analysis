Why it matters

On Meta, the creative decides performance — the same budget wins or loses on the ad itself. Knowing what's working for competitors is the fastest way to sharpen a client's ads, but done by hand it's slow, subjective, and rarely repeated. AdGapIQ makes competitive ad intelligence fast, accurate and repeatable — turning an occasional chore into a standing advantage.
